3/4 cup butter, softened, 1 - 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened, 2 cups sugar, 2 large eggs, 3 cups all purpose flour, 1/2 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 1/2 cups mashed bananas, 4 medium, 1 cup chopped pecans, toasted, 1/2 tsp vanilla

Beat butter and cream cheese until creamy., Gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy., Add eggs 1 at a time., In a small bowl sift dry ingredients., Add slowly to above mixture., Stir in bananas and vanilla., Bake in greased and floured loaf pans, Makes 2 loaves, TOPPING:, Prepare batter as directed and put in pans., Stir together 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar, 1/2 cup chopped, nuts,1 TBS flour, 1 TBS melted butter and 1/8 tsp ground, cinnamon. Sprinkle over batter and bake on 350Â° for 1 hour
