Mee Maw"s BBQ Sauce

Clark family recipe for Mee Maw"s BBQ Sauce.

1/2 Cup Water, 1/2 Cup Vinegar, 3 Tbs. Brown Sugar, 1 Tbs. Worcestershire, 1 Tbs. A1 Sauce and Heinz 57, Enough Ketchup to desired thickness (at least 2 cups)

Mix all ingredients, whisking to mix everything together. Put in, medium size pot and heat, bringing it to a boil. When boiling, turn, down to simmer, stirring to prevent sticking. When thick, it's done!, Add salt and pepper to taste.

Great on chicken and pork. In a hurry? Just mix it all up, pour into the crockpot over chicken breasts or pork tenderloin and be ready for a yummy dinner when you get home from work!!
